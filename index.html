11 months

Wageningen University & Research

Summary The International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition is a worldwide synthetic biology competition for students. I was a part of the Wageningen University and Research team of 2022, for which we designed and developed a living diagnostic for colorectal cancer. In this project I developed the website, viewable at 2022.igem.wiki/wageningen-ur, and was lead of the design team. My research contribution was in the development of a multi-compartmental dynamic model of the colon and microbiome predicting the residence time of living diagnostics in the colon.
